## About Lettermint

***Never worry about email delivery again!***

**Based in the Netherlands**, we ensure your transactional emails and broadcast emails are secure, reliable, and always reach your customers. Built on our own European infrastructure — no US cloud providers involved.

- **[EU-based](https://lettermint.co/european-email)** — hosted in Europe, built in the Netherlands
- **GDPR compliant** — privacy by design, no surprises
- **High deliverability** — inbox, not spam
- **Developer-first** — REST API, SMTP relay, and SDKs for your favorite stack

## Features

- **[Transactional](https://lettermint.co/features/transactional-emails)** — deliver critical emails like password resets and order confirmations
- **[Broadcast](https://lettermint.co/features/broadcast-emails)** — send newsletters, marketing emails, and campaigns
- **[Inbound](https://lettermint.co/features/inbound-emails)** — receive and process incoming emails via webhooks
